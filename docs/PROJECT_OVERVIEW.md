## 1. What is Computiq Academy?

Computiq Academy is an **online and on-site learning platform** built for the Iraqi market. Students learn skills through courses and programs, earn certificates, prove their abilities, and connect with employers — all in one place.

### The Vision

| Pillar | What It Means |
|--------|--------------|
| 🎓 **Learn** | Students take courses and programs (online or in-person) |
| 🏆 **Prove** | Students earn certificates and verified skills |
| 💼 **Connect** | HR professionals search the talent pool to hire graduates |
| 🏛️ **Partner** | Universities track their students on the platform |

---

## 2. Who Will Use It?

The platform serves **four types of users**:

### 🎓 Student (Default Role)
The main user. Every account starts as a student. Students browse courses, enroll, learn, complete quizzes and coding challenges, earn certificates, and build a professional profile.

### 💼 HR Professional
Represents a company or hiring manager. HR users post job listings, search the talent pool, view candidate profiles, and subscribe to hiring plans.

### 🏛️ Organization
Represents a university or institute. Organizations register, track their students' progress, and see how they rank among other institutions.

### 🛡️ Admin
The academy team. Admins manage all content, users, payments, and settings through the admin panel.

## 4. How Roles Work

### Role Switching

Inspired by [eYouth](https://eyouthlearning.com/en/), a single account can hold multiple roles. A top navigation button lets users switch instantly between Student, HR, and Organization dashboards.

### What Each Role Can Access

| Action | Student | HR | Organization | Admin |
|--------|---------|-----|-------------|-------|
| Browse courses/programs | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Buy and access content | ✅ | — | — | ✅ |
| Take quizzes and coding tasks | ✅ | — | — | — |
| Earn certificates | ✅ | — | — | — |
| Comment and rate | ✅ | — | — | — |
| Post jobs | — | ✅ | — | ✅ |
| Search talents | — | ✅ | — | ✅ |
| View students & rankings | — | — | ✅ | ✅ |
| Manage everything | — | — | — | ✅ |

## 5. Courses & Programs

### What's the Difference?

| | Course | Program |
|-|--------|---------|
| **What** | Single topic | Collection of courses under one goal |
| **Example** | "JavaScript Basics" | "Full-Stack Web Dev" (6 courses) |

### Online vs. On-Site

| Aspect | Online | On-Site |
|--------|--------|---------|
| **Delivery** | Video lessons from anywhere | In-person at a location |
| **Payment** | ZainCash (online) | Cash only |
| **Enrollment** | Instant after payment | Register → Admin approval → Attend |
| **Features** | Video player, quizzes, coding | Attendance tracking, acceptance |

### Course Page Contains

Title, summary, what you'll learn, prerequisites, duration, instructor, lessons list, quizzes, certificate info, price, buy button, online/on-site badge, open/closed status, comments & ratings.

### Program Page Contains

Title, summary, objective, target audience, prerequisites, full course list, instructor/partner info, duration, certificate, price, buy button, online/on-site badge, comments & ratings.

## 6. Student Experience

### Dashboard Shows

- 📚 My Courses with progress bars
- 🎯 My Programs with progress
- ⏰ Upcoming deadlines
- 🏅 Certificates
- 🔔 Notifications

### Learning Experience

- **Video Player** — watch lessons with progress tracking
- **Inline Quizzes** — multiple choice and true/false during or after videos
- **Coding Challenges** — write and run code against test cases
- **Attendance** — for on-site: attend, absent, not yet

### Student Profile (LinkedIn-ready)

| Section | What's Included |
|---------|----------------|
| **Basic Info** | Name, image, headline, bio, city, country, phone, email |
| **Education** | University, department, degree, years, status |
| **Skills** | Manual + verified from courses/programs/exams |
| **Learning** | Enrolled/completed courses & programs |
| **Scores** | Total score (online + on-site combined) |
| **Projects** | Title, description, links, GitHub |
| **Career** | CV, preferred role/city, availability |
| **Social** | LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio |

### Exams & Certificates
- **Standalone Exams** — take exams without taking a course to prove a skill
- **Certificates** — downloadable PDF certificate for courses, programs, and exams.

### Engagement & Smart Features
- **AI Guidance Chatbot** — helps students decide what to learn based on goal and budget
- **Community** — simple community layer for student interactions
- **Retention Features** — reminders, progress visibility, and useful notifications
- **Comments & Ratings** — star ratings (1–5) and comments permitted only after purchase.
- **Student Recognition** — student of the month/course features (later engagement phase)

## 10. Payment & Revenue

### Revenue Streams

| Source | Model |
|--------|-------|
| **Online Courses & Programs** | One-time purchase → instant access |
| **HR Plans** | Monthly subscriptions |
| **On-Site Courses** | Cash at venue |

### Online Payment Flow

1. Student clicks **Buy** → checkout page
2. Pays via **ZainCash**
3. Payment verified → **instant access**

### On-Site Payment Flow

1. Student **registers** for on-site course
2. Admin **approves** registration
3. Student pays **cash** at venue
4. Admin marks paid → enrollment active
